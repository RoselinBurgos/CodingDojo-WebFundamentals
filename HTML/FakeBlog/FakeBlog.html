Silicon Valley

"Silicon Valley is an American satirical comedy television series created by Mike Judge, John Altschuler and Dave Krinsky.
The series focuses on five young men who founded a startup company in Silicon Valley.
The series premiered on April 6, 2014 on HBO, and the fifth season premiered on March 25, 2018. On April 12, 2018, it was announced that HBO had renewed the series for a sixth season."
